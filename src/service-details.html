What is SEO and How Does it Work?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, which is the process of optimizing a website to improve its visibility and ranking on search engines like Google. The goal is to increase organic (non-paid) traffic to the site by making it more relevant and authoritative in the eyes of search engines.

  • First place in the search list.
  • More traffic to your website.
  • Better quality customers.

Qulity of traffic is more important than quantity. It is better to have a few visitors who are genuinely interested in your content than a large number of visitors who leave immediately.

SEO is an ongoing process that requires continuous effort and adaptation to changes in search engine algorithms and user behavior. It involves various techniques such as keyword research, on-page optimization, link building, and content creation to improve a website's performance in search results.
